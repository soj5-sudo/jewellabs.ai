Jewellabs.ai is a high-end B2B platform designed to digitize traditional diamond and jewelry businesses that still rely heavily on manual processes. Instead of offering a single tool, it acts as a full digital infrastructure layer that replaces fragmented systems with one integrated solution.
At the front, it presents an immersive, 3D website experience that positions the business as premium and modern. This is not just visual, it's meant to increase trust and conversion for high-value clients.
Behind the scenes, the platform builds core systems that run the business. It includes AI voice agents that answer incoming calls, hold natural conversations, extract key details like client needs and urgency, and automatically send structured summaries via email. This removes the need for constant human handling of inquiries.
A major component is compliance automation. The system processes invoices from email, extracts required fields using OCR and AI, and generates legally compliant G7 Due Diligence Statement PDFs. If data is missing, it flags issues for review. This ensures businesses meet upcoming international regulations without manual work.
It also includes an AI-powered design engine that can generate product visuals, marketing content, and concepts from simple prompts, helping businesses create assets quickly without design teams.
All of this is wrapped in a subscription model with ongoing maintenance, updates, and scaling support. Payments are integrated across traditional and crypto methods.
In short, Jewellabs.ai transforms legacy jewelry businesses into automated, compliant, and digitally advanced operations that can compete at a global level.
